\problemsec

State the definition of prime ideals.


\begin{solution}
As in \parencite[126]{hungerford}, an ideal $P\subset R$ is prime if for any ideal $A,B \subset R$,
\[
    AB\subset R\implies A\subset R \Or B\subset R
\]
\end{solution}
